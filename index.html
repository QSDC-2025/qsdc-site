About Us

We're a non-profit community of dance lovers based in the Quinte Region, where the joy of movement and music brings people together. Most of our members are seniors, but dancers of all ages and experience levels are always welcome. We host regular social dances featuring Live bands - with a toe-tapping mix that leans toward country but spans a wide range of musical styles. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or just looking to try something new, come join us on the dance floor!
